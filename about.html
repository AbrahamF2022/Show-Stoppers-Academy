Our Mission

Help us expand our outreach!

Show Stoppers Academy (“SSA”) is a community-based group that will provide a “Safe Haven” for our youth's future leaders. A place where the youth from all walks of life can build confidence, strength, and camaraderie through sports and education.

We currently live in a day and age where social acceptance supersedes humanity. At SSA, we provide our children with a safe space to be themselves, encourage them to have respect for their peers and elders, and create a space to develop into productive individuals. This is all accomplished through education in financial literacy, health and wellness, and various travel opportunities through sports.
