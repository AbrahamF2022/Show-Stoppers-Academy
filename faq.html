Common Questions

Quick answers to help you get started with Show Stoppers Academy.

You can enroll your child by filling out our registration form online or contacting us directly. We'll provide you with all the necessary information about program schedules, requirements, and fees. Our registration process is simple and we're here to help every step of the way.

We serve youth from ages 5 to 18, with programs tailored to different age groups and skill levels. Our programs are designed to grow with your child as they develop. We have specific age brackets for different activities to ensure appropriate development and safety.

Yes, we believe every child should have access to our programs regardless of financial circumstances. We offer scholarships and payment plans based on need. Contact us to learn more about our financial assistance options and how to apply.

We welcome volunteers in many capacities! You can help with coaching, administrative tasks, event planning, or mentoring. Contact us to discuss volunteer opportunities and complete our volunteer application process. We'll match your skills and interests with our needs.

Safety is our top priority. All our staff and volunteers are background-checked and trained in safety protocols. We maintain proper equipment, have first aid certified personnel on-site, and follow strict safety guidelines for all activities. We also require parental consent and emergency contact information for all participants.

In addition to sports programs, we offer educational workshops covering financial literacy, health and wellness, leadership development, and life skills. We also organize community service projects, cultural events, and provide mentorship opportunities to help youth develop holistically.

You can stay updated by following us on social media, subscribing to our newsletter, or checking our website regularly. We also send email updates to registered families about upcoming events, program changes, and special opportunities.
