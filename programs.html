Educational Programs

Complementary educational initiatives that support academic success and life skills development.

Financial Literacy

Teaching youth about budgeting, saving, investing, and making smart financial decisions for their future.

  • • Budgeting and money management
  • • Understanding credit and debt
  • • Basic investment concepts
  • • College financial planning

Health & Wellness

Promoting physical and mental health through nutrition education, stress management, and healthy lifestyle choices.

  • • Nutrition and healthy eating
  • • Mental health awareness
  • • Stress management techniques
  • • Physical fitness and recovery

Leadership Development

Building confidence, communication skills, and leadership abilities that will serve youth throughout their lives.

  • • Public speaking and communication
  • • Team leadership skills
  • • Goal setting and planning
  • • Conflict resolution
